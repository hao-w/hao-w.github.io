    I am a Ph.D candidate in the Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University. My supervisor is Prof. Jan-Willem van de Meent. Previously, I received my M.Sc in Computer Science at University of Virginia. Also I received an M.Sc in Applied Mathematics from University of Washington.


    My main research focus is in deep probabilistic modeling and probabilistic programming. I am interested in developing scalable inference methods for deep generative models, in which I hope to learn representations that can uncover hierarchical structure of data in various problem domains.
