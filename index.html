Curriculum Vitae

I am a Ph.D student in the Probabilistic Modeling Group at Northeastern University. My advisor is Prof. Jan-Willem van de Meent. My main research focus on amortized inferece, deep probabilistic models and probabilistic programming.

Prior to joining Northeastern, I received my M.Sc in Computer Science at University of Virginia in 2016. I also have an M.Sc in Applied Mathematics from University of Washington.
